
-- What is the CIA Triangle --

· Common Security Model that is used to guide policies that are put in place
· This is also the case when revisiting older policies


-- The CIA Triangle --

· Confidentiality
· Integrity
· Availability


-- Confidentiality --

· Controls the access to information
· Put in place to ensure only people with correct access has access
· Commonly called Authorization
· Good example is 2FA


-- Integrity --

· This ensures the data accessed is trusted and accurate at all times
· Data that is not consistent and inaccurate breaks the triangle
· Example is Data Encryption


-- Availability --

· If the data is unavailable then it is useless
· Common cause of this breaking the triangle is a DDOS attack
· Disaster Recovery Plan is crucial for this step so everything can be mitigated and brought up quickly
